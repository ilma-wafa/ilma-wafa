## Who I Am

I'm a Software Engineering graduate (BEng Hons, London Metropolitan University) building a career in Quality Assurance. I care about quality being engineered into products — not just finding bugs, but preventing them from shipping in the first place.

My work sits at the intersection of QA engineering and AI. I build tools that make testing smarter, write documentation that makes systems auditable, and I believe a well-written test plan is just as important as the code it covers.
